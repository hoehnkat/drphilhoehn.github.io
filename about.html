About Dr. Philip Hoehn

Dr. Philip M. Hoehn graduated from the Logan College of Chiropractic and has completed graduate work in Industrial Consultation and Ergonomics. He is a member of The International Academy of Low Back Pain Study, and a diplomat of The National Board of Chiropractic Examiners.

In addition to affiliations already mentioned, Dr. Hoehn is also involved in many community services. He is a consultant to business and industry and team physician for various sports teams.

Dr. Hoehn finished his post-graduate work in sports injury. This allows him to apply for one of two positions as Team Chiropractor for the U.S. Olympic Team.

Dr. Hoehn is father to three amazing children, all former athletes, and loving grandparent to one.
