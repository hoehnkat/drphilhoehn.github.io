Our Office

Hours of Operation

For the convenience of our patients, the Livonia clinic is open Monday through Saturday.

  • Monday/Wednesday/Friday: 8am – 6pm
  • Tuesday/Thursday: 9am – noon, afternoons by appointment
  • Saturday: 9am – noon

Should an emergency arise Dr. Hoehn may be reached during office hours through his answering service.

 

Appointments

In order to devote adequate time to each patient, visits are by appointment only. The need and frequency of your visits will depend on the nature of each problem and may vary from patient to patient.

If for some reason you are unable to keep your appointment, please notify the office as soon as possible for rescheduling. To cancel, change or make appointments, we would appreciate your calling during office hours.

Please be advised that we reserve the right to charge a fee for appointments not kept or cancelled within a reasonable time.

 

Waiting Time

We realize that your time is valuable, so ever effort has been made to shorten the time you have to wait to see the doctor. Occasionally, however, there may be a short delay due to emergencies or unexpected procedures.

We as you to bear with us so that each patient may have as much time with the doctor as necessary.

 

Fees

You will be pleased to know that Chiropractic care is covered by most insurance companies, including workers compensation, auto no-fault and Medicare. We know that financial problems arise once in a while, and it is our desire to work with you to help you through any sudden crisis that may occur.

We believe that open communication regarding fees and charges leads to better patient-doctor relationships. Your comments and questions are encouraged at all times in the hope of eliminating unnecessary confusion.

Please be advised that any balance over three months old will be charged a five percent interest.
